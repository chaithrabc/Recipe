DHOKLA

Dhokla has its origins in the Indian state of Gujarat, where it has been enjoyed for centuries as a traditional snack. It has long been a staple in Gujarati cuisine and is now popular across India and worldwide. It gained recognition as a healthy and flavorful snack. Its popularity has spread beyond Gujarat, becoming a beloved dish in Indian households and a favorite street food item. Dhokla's simplicity, versatility, and nutritional benefits have contributed to its enduring popularity throughout history.
